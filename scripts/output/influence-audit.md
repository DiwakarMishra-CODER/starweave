# Starweave Influence Audit

Generated 2026-07-07. Research method: web search across Wikipedia, AllMusic, band-member interviews, and reputable music press (Rolling Stone, NME, Far Out, Pitchfork-adjacent outlets, KEXP/Stereogum interviews, etc.) for the 46 current graph artists plus 10 confirmed candidate additions — **Built to Spill, Modest Mouse, Blur, Stereolab, Fugazi, Minor Threat, Fontaines D.C., IDLES, Can, Neu!** (56 artists total).

Every entry below has a real source URL. Critic "sounds like" comparisons and genre-logic inferences were explicitly excluded, even when found during research — if no one on record said "X influenced me," it isn't listed.

**Totals: 72 proposed new edges · 23 existing edges with no documented source · 37 external node candidates cited by 2+ artists.**
